New Little Lemon's Menu

At Little Lemon, we blend Italian, Greek, and Turkish culinary traditions to offer a rotating seasonal menu in Chicago. Our family-owned restaurant emphasizes fresh, flavorful food and a welcoming, rustic atmosphere, providing high-quality meals at moderate prices. Breakfast (7:00 AM – 11:00 AM): Start your day with Mediterranean Omelette, Shakshuka, or Greek Yogurt Parfait. Lunch (11:30 AM – 2:30 PM): Enjoy refreshing options like Chicken Souvlaki Wraps, Falafel & Hummus Plate, or a Caprese Salad. Dinner (5:00 PM – 10:00 PM): Conclude your day with comforting dishes such as Lamb Kofta with Couscous, Seafood Risotto, or Eggplant Moussaka.
